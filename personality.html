WHAT IS MY STRONGEST TRAIT AND HOW DOES IT IMPACT MY ACADEMIC LIFE

My strongest trait I would say is RESILIENCE.It is really important to my academic path. My resilience in the face of difficulties—whether they be challenging coursework, pressing deadlines, or personal setbacks—has been invaluable. Overcoming Difficulties: Rather than giving up on a difficult subject in class, I face it head-on. For instance, I spent additional hours practicing during a particularly difficult arithmetic lesson and asked my teacher for assistance. This perseverance gave me more confidence in addition to helping me understand the subject. Resilience aids in my ability to stay focused during difficult times, like test weeks. I tell myself that obstacles are transitory and that I am capable of overcoming them. I can successfully manage my time and stay organized with this approach, so that I complete assignments and study for tests without succumbing to stress.

MY HERO

When I reflect on the concept of a hero, the answer that springs to mind may seem typical or cliché to many: my mother. Yet, for me, she embodies the essence of heroism in ways that transcend the ordinary. She is not just a caregiver; she is a guiding light, a source of unwavering support, and my closest confidante. From the very beginning, my mother has given me the world. She sacrificed her own comforts to provide me with opportunities—an education, a warm home, and the freedom to pursue my dreams. Her selflessness knows no bounds; she has always put my needs above her own, often at great personal cost. Yet, what truly sets her apart as my hero is not just her role as a mother but also our profound friendship. In a world that can sometimes feel isolating and overwhelming, my mother is my unofficial best friend—the one person I can always turn to. Whether I’m celebrating a small victory or grappling with life’s challenges, my first instinct is “Let me tell my mother,” I think, knowing that her words and comfort will do the job. . She is more than just a parent; she is my favorite girl. As I navigate life’s journey, I carry her spirit with me, grateful for the love that shapes who I am today.In celebrating my mother as my hero, I recognize that this connection is not just about admiration but also about gratitude for the profound impact she has had on my life. She is a testament to the power of love—a love that knows no limits and continues to inspire me every day. Which leads me to her favorite quote "PRAY AND LEAVE THE REST TO GOD" which i use as a life guide in whatever i do.

Simple Minds Personality Test- REFLECTION

Like many personality tests, the validity of the Simple Minds Personality Test is frequently questioned because of problems with construct validity and reliability. Since responses might change depending on environment or emotion, many tests struggle to produce consistent findings across time, which is a need for validity. Furthermore, it is frequently unknown how accurate the test is, and respondents may falsify their responses in order to appear more positive. Because of this, the Simple Minds Personality Test should be considered one of many instruments for self-reflection rather than a comprehensive assessment of personality, even though it may offer some insights into personality traits due to its poor overall validity.
